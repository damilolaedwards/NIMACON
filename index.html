NIMACON 2019

18th - 22nd November, 2019

Main Auditorium, University of Ilorin, Ilorin, Kwara - Nigeria.

Introduction

The Materials Science and Technology Society of Nigeria (MSN) in collaboration with top materials related organizations and institutions in Nigeria, is organising her annual Congress and Workshop tagged NIMACON2019 on the theme: SOURCING AND MANAGEMENT OF MATERIAL RESOURCES FOR SUSTAINABLE ECONOMIC DIVERSIFICATION, as it affects the strategic areas of some National Economy. The congress would focus on materials resources, conventional and advanced secondary raw materials development technologies/activities around the world and implications on sustainable development of the Economy. The Congress is thus, planned to be a forum whereby Materials-related professionals in the fields of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Electronics, Materials Science, Metallurgy, Agriculture, Building Technology, Engineering, Biological Sciences, Biotechnology, Geology, among other diverse disciplines in Nigeria and from around the world would discuss the theme of the congress. Special workshops will also be featured.

 

CALL FOR PAPERS

Participants wishing to present papers are invited to register online at http://conference.msn.ng and submit electronic copies of an abstract of not more than 300 words single line spacing, clearly stating the title of the paper, name(s) of the author(s) and the affiliation(s) including phone numbers and emails. All abstracts should reach the COC Secretariat latest Sunday 30th September, 2018. Acceptance letter for oral or poster presentation shall be sent to Author within one week from the date of receipt of abstract. Soft copies of full length papers in MS Word format of accepted abstract plus the evidence of payment of the conference fee should reach the Secretariat not later than Sunday 14th October, 2018. English will be the official language of this conference.

Submit abstract and full paper by E-mail attachment to nimacon.ilorin2019@gmail.com
